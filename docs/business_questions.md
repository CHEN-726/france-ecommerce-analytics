# Business Questions

This project is designed to answer a set of business questions relevant to a France-focused D2C e-commerce company.

## Main Objective

The goal is to understand where business growth comes from, where performance is under pressure, and what actions management should prioritise in the next quarter.

---

## 1. Executive / Management Questions

### Q1. Which French cities are the strongest growth engines?
This question aims to identify which markets contribute the most to revenue growth and which cities should receive additional attention or investment.

### Q2. Which cities underperform relative to their potential or targets?
This helps detect markets where traffic exists but commercial efficiency remains weak.

### Q3. Is performance improvement driven by volume, conversion, or basket value?
This question helps distinguish whether growth is coming from:
- more traffic
- better conversion
- higher average order value

---

## 2. Customer Funnel Questions

### Q4. Where does the customer funnel leak the most?
The project evaluates user progression across the main funnel stages:
- View
- Favorite
- Add to Cart
- Purchase

### Q5. How does funnel performance vary by city, channel and device?
This helps reveal whether performance issues are local, channel-related or behavioral.

### Q6. Are returning users significantly more efficient than new users?
This question helps assess whether retention and CRM actions deserve stronger priority.

---

## 3. Customer Value Questions

### Q7. Which customer segments contribute most to business stability and revenue?
The project uses customer-level metrics such as recency, frequency and monetary value to identify high-priority groups.

### Q8. Which customer groups should receive immediate CRM attention?
This includes:
- high-value customers
- potential loyalists
- one-time buyers
- at-risk customers

---

## 4. Product & Market Questions

### Q9. Which categories are true revenue drivers and which ones mainly generate traffic?
This helps distinguish commercial contribution from simple browsing interest.

### Q10. Which category-city combinations show the strongest growth opportunities?
This is important for market-specific commercial actions and promotional planning.

---

## 5. Strategic Questions

### Q11. Where should management reallocate budget in the next quarter?
The analysis should help support decisions on:
- market prioritisation
- CRM focus
- category support
- conversion improvement

### Q12. What should be the business priorities for the next 30 / 60 / 90 days?
The final objective is to move from analysis to action.

---

## Expected Output

The project is expected to produce:

- a clear executive overview
- a city-level performance diagnosis
- a funnel conversion diagnosis
- a customer segmentation framework
- a business-oriented action plan for management
